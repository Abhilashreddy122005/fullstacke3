About Veltech University

Established with Vision for Excellence

Veltech University, also known as Rangarajan Dr. Sagunthala R&D Institute of Science and Technology, is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality education in the fields of engineering, technology, and research.

  • Placement assistance and career guidance
